What we're about

Uniting developers, designers, and product managers with nonprofits, local governments, and other civic causes to make a positive impact on Montana communities.

Code for Montana is a Code for America Brigade focused on improving the lives of residents of Montana. Our goal is to combine our expertise to build technology or provide technology education to make a tangible difference in the services nonprofits and local governments are able to offer.

Even if you are new to tech, there may be opportunities for you to contribute by making connections and helping to find and arrange projects. No coding experience is required.

Please join us in the #code-for-montana Slack channel in the Missoula Tech workspace.
